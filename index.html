The Official Brookline High School App

Is school hard enough? Why struggle searching through emails and websites for your schedule. Why struggle to find you classmate's email. Instead, use The Official Brookline High School App!

Manage your classes and converse with friends in the smae class. Create events and make announcements. It's all now feasible through one app - just one click away!

The Official Brookline High School App is social media platform that allows you to easily make friends, communicate with friends, organize your schedule, and post announcements.

When submitting a friend request, your email and phone number will be given.

This app does not have proper regulation, so students should be self-aware of what they post.

Under most circumstances, we will not give any texts over to adminstraors.
